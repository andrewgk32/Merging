How It Works:

At the core of our virtual try-on solution lies a powerful combination of computer vision and machine learning algorithms. Leveraging OpenCV and dlib, two leading libraries in the field, our code captures video frames from a webcam, processes them, and performs facial landmark detection. OpenCV handles video frame capture and pre-processing tasks such as resizing and converting frames to grayscale. Meanwhile, dlib's pre-trained facial landmark detection model identifies and localizes key facial features, including the eyes, nose, and mouth. These facial landmarks serve as reference points for fitting the chosen pair of glasses onto the user's face in real-time. By seamlessly integrating these technologies, we provide a user-friendly and accurate virtual try-on experience that brings online eyewear shopping to life.
